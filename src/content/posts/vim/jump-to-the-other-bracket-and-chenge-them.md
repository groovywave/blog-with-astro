jump to the other bracket
Copy and paste the below text and type some commands like below.

---
1. /TIP5 4 2 <CR> 
2. j 0 % r]       (jump the other bracket and replace it)
3. '' r[          (jump to original position and replace it)
---
TIP542 
{London, New\ York, Berlin}
